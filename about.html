What is the White Hat Hackers Club?

The White Hat Hackers club is dedicated to exploring the vast domain of cybersecurity. Their focus areas include digital forensics, vulnerability assessment and penetration testing (VAPT), cryptography, networking, and other related domains. The club is committed to hands-on learning and provides practical experience to its members by utilizing various tools. The use of such tools not only broadens the members' knowledge horizons but also enhances their learning experience.

When do we come in action?

We come together to mentor and enlighten you about the cyber crimes and cybersecurity around the world.

What do we do?

We create an environment where one can learn from another in a positive workspace while working on our skills to provide a better security for a better tomorrow.

Our Mission

●To produce talented Certified Ethical Hackers.
● Spread awareness about the cyber crimes and cybersecurity occurring and how to be safe from them.
● To conduct exciting events to make learning more enthusiastic.
● To create an environment where students can learn from one another in a positive workspace.
